Change phone number

What it sounds like

Customers asking to change the phone number on their account.

What to say and do

  1. Make sure you are speaking with either the account holder or an authorized contact person listed on the account.
  2. If the customer has more than one account, ask if they would like to change the phone number for all of their accounts or only one/some of them.
  3. Change the phone number. [Show me how: Change all] or [Show me how: Change one/some]

Update ID information

What it sounds like

Customers asking to update their ID information (state-issued ID, Social Security Number, etc.).

What to say and do

  1. Make sure you are speaking with either the account holder or an authorized contact person listed on the account.
  2. Verify the ID information.
    Type of ID How to handle it
    Texas ID or driver license Verify in SAP
    Other state ID or driver license Verify in Accurint
    US passport or Mexican consular ID card (Matricula Consular/Mexican CID card) Let our customer know that they must come to one of our customer service centers
  3. If the verification is successful, update the customer's ID information. [Show me how]

Add an authorized contact person

What it sounds like

Customers asking to add an authorized person, such as a spouse or roommate, to the account.

What to say and do

  1. Make sure you are speaking with either the account holder or an authorized contact person listed on the account.
  2. Make sure the customer understands that, once added, this person will have full account access and be able to conduct business transactions on the account.
  3. In Accurint, verify the authorized contact's identity by checking one of these:
    • State-issued drivers license or ID number (check Texas DL numbers in SAP)
    • Social Security number
  4. Add the authorized contact person. [Show me how]

Remove an authorized contact person

What it sounds like

Customers asking to remove an authorized person from the account.

What to say and do

  1. Make sure you are speaking with either the account holder or an authorized contact person listed on the account.
  2. Remove the authorized contact person. [Show me how]

Lost or stolen ID / identity theft

What it sounds like

Customers calling to inform us that:

  • Their ID/driver's license is lost/stolen
  • They believe their identity has been stolen

What to say and do

  1. Sorry to hear that.
  2. Make sure you are speaking with either the account holder or an authorized contact person listed on the account.
  3. Say something like, "We need to create a new password on your account to keep it secure." Change the account password. If it doesn't have one, create one. [Show me how]
  4. If customer says their identity has been stolen, encourage them to file a police report. They will need the police report information if they intend to deny responsibility for charges (all of the charges caused by the identity thief).
  5. Create a contact. Class 115 - Change Customer Data, action code 0009 Service Denial-Password -Lost/Stolen IDs
